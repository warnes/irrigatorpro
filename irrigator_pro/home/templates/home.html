What Is IrrigatorPro?

IrrigatorPro provides growers of peanuts, corn and cotton with a simple tool to determine when to irrigate to ensure optimal crop growth while minimizing irrigation costs.

These irrigation recommendations are based on long-term, multicrop irrigation management research performed by the ARS National Peanut Research Laboratory in Dawson, Georgia.

More Information »

How Does It Work?

IrrigatorPro calculates the available water in the soil and the daily water needs of the crop based on its growth stage.

If the water needs of the crop exceed the avilable water in the soil, irrigation is recommended.

IrrigatorPro can calculate the available soil water content using

  • manually entered rainfall and irrigaton data,
  • manually entered values from soil water potential sensors, or
  • soil water potential sensor values wirelessly transmitted to the University of Georgia (UGA) data repository.

What Crops Are Supported?

IrrigatorPro currently supports the following crops:

  • Corn
  • Cotton
  • Peanuts
